
Structure of table 'vendas':
- id (int(11) unsigned)
- empresa_id (int(11) unsigned)
- user_id (int(11) unsigned)
- total_amount (decimal(10,2))
- payment_method (varchar(50))
- created_at (timestamp)

- id (int(11) unsigned)

- quantity (int(11))
- unit_price (decimal(10,2))
